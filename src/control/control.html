Shortcuts & tips
  • Space — play / pause. R — reset to top. ↑ / ↓ — speed ± 5. L — toggle click-through. Esc — close prompter.
  • When the prompter is locked (click-through on), the HUD still works because we forward mouse events to interactive children. The reading area itself is fully click-through.
  • Drag the bottom-right corner of the floating window to resize live. Position preset recenters it.
